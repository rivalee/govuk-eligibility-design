New State Pension

Your State Pension is money to help with living costs. It depends on your National Insurance record.

How much

Up to £203.85 a week

When to claim

You’ll get a letter telling you when you can claim.

Find out more about new State Pension (opens in new tab)

Pension Credit

Pension Credit gives you extra money to help with your living costs.

How much

It depends on your situation and is paid every 4 weeks

When to claim

Up to 4 months before you reach State Pension age.

Find out more about Pension Credit (opens in new tab)

Housing Benefit

Housing Benefit can help you pay your rent.

How much

It depends on your situation

When to claim

In advance by up to 13 weeks (or 17 weeks if you’re aged 60 or over)

Find out more about Housing Benefit (opens in new tab)

Carer's Allowance

Carer's Allowance is financial support if you provide care for someone and they get certain benefits

How much

£76.75 a week paid either weekly in advance or every 4 weeks

When to claim

At any point and you can backdate your claim by up to 3 months.

Find out more about Carer's Allowance (opens in new tab)

Attendance Allowance

Attendance Allowance helps with extra costs if you have a disability severe enough that you need someone to help look after you.

How much

£68.10 or £101.75 a week

When to claim

At any time

Find out more about Attendance Allowance (opens in new tab)

Carer's Credit

Carer’s Credit is a National Insurance credit that helps with gaps in your National Insurance record.

How much

National Insurance credits

When to claim

You’ll get it automatically if you’re eligible

Find out more about Carer's Credit (opens in new tab)

Warm Home Discount Scheme

Warm Home Discount Scheme is a one-off discount applied to your electricity bill.

How much

£150 off your electricity bill between early October 2023 and 31 March 2024

When to claim

You get it automatically if you're eligible

Find out more about Warm Home Discount Scheme (opens in a new tab)

Cold Weather Payment

You may get Cold Weather Payments if you’re getting certain benefits and live in England, Wales or Northern Ireland.

How much

£25 for each 7 day period of very cold weather between 1 November 2023 and 31 March 2024.

When to claim

You get it automatically if you're eligible

Find out more about Cold Weather Payment (opens in a new tab)
